The Quinta

Located 3km from the town of Seia, in the western foothills of the Serra da Estrela Natural Park, this beautifully restored farmstead has a decidedly homely feel, that will make you not want to leave.

Farmed by the Ferrão family since 1935, the Quinta produces a wide variety of homegrown produce, whilst our small vineyard contributes grapes to the local wine cooperative.

Whether you want to relax by the swimming pool with a snack from the BBQ or explore the surrounding mountains, this is a place to recharge your batteries and feel at home.

Accommodation

The accomodation is composed of three separate buildings. The main farm house, Casa da Eira, has 5 doubles and 1 twin available. Casa do Azeite and Casa do Queijo are two separate farm buildings with space for for 2 - 4 people.

Casa da Eira

Completely renovated in 2016, the main farm house has five double rooms with ensuite bathroom, TV and air conditioning. A sixth twin room can be connected up to one of the double rooms for family groups.

The large dining room boasts a real fire and modern kitchen facilities, providing a relaxing space for eating and socialising with other guests.

Region

The Quinta is an ideal base from which to discover the Serra da Estrela, Portugal's highest mountain range.

With its bizarrely shaped crags and gorges, mountain streams and lakes, beautiful forests and magnificent views, the region is one of Portugal’s most outstanding scenic attractions.

The park is excellent for trekking, horse-riding, and cycling with some 375km of marked trails of varying degrees of difficulty.

In winter, Serra da Estrela is the only place in Portugal where you can ski, offering several lift served pistes.

Make sure you try the most famous product in the region - the Serra da Estrela cheese. With its buttery texture, it goes ideally with the traditional local bread.
